Nutrition

Dialed‑in Diet Plans

We keep it simple, science‑based, and tasty. Three paths — pick what matches your goals.

Weight Loss

Cut Smart, Not Joy

High protein, controlled kcal, fiber‑loaded plates. Weekly refeeds to stay sane.

  • ~500 kcal deficit • 1.6–2.2g protein/kg
  • Plates: Grilled chicken, brown rice, veggies
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt, fruit, nuts
Muscle Gain

Lean Bulk Protocol

Slight surplus, protein‑timing around workouts, creatine & hydration.

  • +250–300 kcal • 2–2.4g protein/kg
  • Plates: Paneer/tofu, rotis, dal, ghee
  • Pre/Post: Banana + whey (optional)
Balanced

Everyday Athlete

Sustainable macros with flexible meals you’ll actually enjoy.

  • ~Maintenance kcal • 30/30/40 (P/F/C)
  • Plates: Eggs, rice, veggies, fish/chicken
  • Weekend treats accounted for
